Sleep patterns of 2019

I started collecting data on my sleep habits about 3 years ago when I noticed that my sleep schedule was becoming increasingly irregular. I had thought that by seeing my sleep patterns fall into disarray in real time I would make a conscious effort to correct it.

And yet, here we are, 3 years later, with very little progress. I realized that sleep will always be something that I struggle to value, but I'm working on prioritizing it for the upcoming year.

I took very different classes during my spring and fall semesters at university. It's interesting to see how my sleep schedule was more consistent in the spring and how I tried to skew my sleep times earlier in the fall. You can also catch glimpses of when my poor sleep habits relapsed in fall.
